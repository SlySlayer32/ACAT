ACAT Online Form Draft Answers

This document is a drafting aid for the ACAT online form titled Application for Interim or Other Orders - General.
Copy and adapt the wording below into the form fields.
Do not submit this page as evidence unless needed as part of Filing Pack 1.

[Order sought]
1. The applicants seek an order varying the current termination date of 31 March 2026 to 28 April 2026.
2. The applicants seek leave to remain in occupation of the premises until 28 April 2026 on the existing tenancy terms, including continued payment of rent.
3. The applicants are willing for the property to be advertised immediately and for inspections or viewings to occur on reasonable notice during the extension period.

[Grounds relied on]
1. The applicants are a family with three children and have 13 years of continuous rental history.
2. The applicants have been actively seeking alternative accommodation but have not yet secured a suitable property, as shown by their rental application material.
3. The applicants have the financial capacity to continue paying rent during the short extension sought, as shown by their Centrelink income, budget, and contract income material.
4. The key hardship issue is that the applicants' 9-year-old daughter has a long-awaited autism assessment booked for 31 March 2026, being the same date as the current termination date.
5. The applicants say they have spent years trying to obtain that assessment and that if it is missed, a further lengthy delay is likely.
6. The timing of the assessment makes it impracticable to complete the move, arrange removalists, and return the property properly on the same date.
7. The extension sought is short, specific, and proportionate. It would allow the applicants to complete the assessment, secure alternative accommodation, arrange removalists and cleaning, and vacate in an orderly way.
8. The prejudice to the landlord is limited because the applicants are willing to cooperate with advertising and inspections during the extension period.

[Attachment note]
The applicants rely on the attached statement and exhibits, including the medical hardship material, financial capacity material, rental application material, and payment-compliance material.
